About Us

Who we Are

Commercials & Co. is your #1 source for the latest commercial and casting news for all of Hollywood’s biggest and most exciting productions. If your child is an up and coming performer between the ages of 3 months to 15 years, Commercials & Co. can help you find the latest commercial and casting notices from the world of film, television and modeling. If it’s happening in entertainment, you’ll find it on Commercials & Co.!

What we do

Commercials & Co. features commercial and casting notices from such television networks as ABC, NBC, The Disney Channel and Nickelodeon. We also promote our work as a place where actors can connect with the greater performing arts community. More importantly, Commercials & Co. is where our clients can get all the information they need to know for their child to succeed in the entertainment business.

When do we operate

Auditions and commercial calls are updated DAILY so check back often for all of our exciting notices. Commercials & Co. publishes between 800 to 1,000 top audition notices each week for every type of TV commercials: informative, testimonial advertising, TV, sitcom series, and more.

Services we offer

The services currently being offered includes browsing through our commercial notices, taking a look at our featured child actors and getting a rough estimate of the cost of how much your child can score from starring in our feature TV commercials.
